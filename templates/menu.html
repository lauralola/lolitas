About our Menu

A new eating experience

  • How it Started

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Inspired by her Grandmother, Lolita, our head chef Adrianna began this project with a view for using local, sustainable produce. Lolita farmed a small patch of land with vegetables and from this created beautiful and tasty meals for her large extended family

  • Sustainabilty

    A focus on a lower impact

    Our menu changes daily and is a provided tasting menu. Our booking form will ask for any dietary requirements and from this we will create your meals using local, sustainable produce. This keeps our carbon footprint low and promotes sustainable eating practices.

  • The Food

    Five Courses

    We provide an appetiser, starter, main and desert each day inspired by our local market ingredients and catering to dietary requirements
